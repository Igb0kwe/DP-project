RUBY SPRINGFIELD COLLEGE ANTHEM

From far and near,
we come to you,
dear Ruby Springfield College,
great potter of mind and body,
to you we yield ourselves to mould.

Great citadel of supreme execellence,
shine on - shine on, you Ruby Stars,
the sky will be our starting point,
to you we yield ourselves to mould.

Alma mater,
hive of learning,
camp of patroits and good breeding,
we cherish you,
and to you we offer
to shine for you forever.

Great citadel of supreme execellence,
shine on - shine on you Ruby Stars,
the sky will be our starting point,
to you we yield ourselves to mould.
